Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, any relevant medical reports, and your insurance details if applicable.

Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?

No, you don’t need a referral. You can book an appointment directly with us.

How long is a typical session?

Sessions usually last between 30-60 minutes, depending on your treatment plan.
